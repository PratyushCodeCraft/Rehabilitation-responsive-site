About Us

Homepage > About Us

At Hazaribagh Jeevan Rehab Foundation, we believe recovery is not just about overcoming addiction—it's about rebuilding lives with dignity, hope, and strength.

Founded with a mission to serve the people of Jharkhand and beyond, our center provides a safe, supportive, and structured environment for individuals battling alcohol and drug dependency. We combine medical care, counseling, therapy, and lifestyle practices like yoga and meditation to ensure holistic healing.

Our dedicated team of doctors, counselors, and support staff work round the clock to guide every individual through a personalized recovery journey. We are more than a rehabilitation centre—we are a family that walks with you, step by step, toward a healthier, addiction-free future.
