Homepage > Alcohol De-Addiction Program

Alcohol addiction takes a serious toll on mental, physical, and emotional health. At Hazaribagh Jeevan Rehab Centre, we offer structured, medical detox and rehabilitation plans for individuals battling alcoholism. With counseling, fitness therapy, and round-the-clock care, we help patients rebuild a sober, meaningful life.

  • Yoga, meditation, and stress-relief programs
  • Balanced nutrition and doctor-prescribed meals
  • Private and semi-private accommodation options
  • Trained psychologists and de-addiction experts
  • Regular progress tracking and feedback sessions
  • Secure, peaceful campus with nature access
